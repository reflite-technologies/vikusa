Welcome to

vihiga county kisii university students association

our core values are:

Networking Opportunities

Join a vibrant community that encourages networking and building lasting friendships among peers from similar backgrounds. We actively engage in community service initiatives, partnering with local organizations to support various causes that uplift our community and foster goodwill.

Academic support

Access resources and mentorship programs designed to help students excel academically and navigate university life successfully. We are looking fpward to establish scholarship programs aimed at assisting students from vihiga county who face financial hardship, ensuring that education remains accessible.

Social events

participate in various social events that promote networking and foster friendships with fellow students. Join us for our annual meetings and other social functions such as hikes and sports tournaments. These events are a great opportunity to connect with fellow students and celebrate our diversity.
