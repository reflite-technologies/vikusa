welcome to VIKUSA

Connecting Vihiga County Students at Kisii University

The birth of VIKUSA

14th November 2017

VIKUSA was founded to create a supportive network for students from Vihiga county at kisii University. Recognizing the unique challenges faced by these students, we aimed to foster a sense of belonging and community. Our early meetings focused on understanding the needs of our members and laying the groundwork of future initiatives.

Our Mission

14th November 2017

To unite, empower and represent Vihiga County students at Kisii University, fostering academic excellence, social welfare and community development, while promoting the county's rich cultural heritage and values

Our Vision

14th November 2017

To be the leading students association in kenya, driving transformative leadership, innovation and socio-economic development for the betterment of Vihiga County and beyond.

Our History

VIKUSA was established in 2017 by a group of passionate students from Vihiga County who recognized the need for a u nified platform to address the unique challenges and opportunities facing students from their home county at Kisii University.

What started as a small group of 25 founding members has grown into a thriving association with over 250 active members across various academic disciplines. Over the years, VIKUSA has become a model student organization, known for its academic achievements, cultural programs, and community service initiatives.

Today, VIKUSA continues to be a beacon of unity, academic excellence, and cultural pride, preparing students not just for successful careers but for meaningful contributions to society
